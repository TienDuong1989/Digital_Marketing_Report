February Traffic Drop — Explained
Why Feb users dropped −50% but leads hit 31 (best month)
Jan LinkedIn Ads ($1,962) drove high traffic but low-quality users. When Ads stopped in Feb, traffic dropped but lead quality improved → CVR spiked to 0.94%.
Jan — High traffic, low CVR 6,629 users · 0.41% CVR
Feb — Low traffic, high CVR ✅ 3,286 users · 0.94% CVR
Mar — Core Update hit both 4,486 users · 0.42% CVR
